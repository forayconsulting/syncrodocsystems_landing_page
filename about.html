Our story

This started with a real merger and a real deadline.

In the fall of 2025, a pension fund merger put five inherited plans on the table. Hundreds of pages of governing language, dozens of amendments, and a legal team that needed to reconcile all of it by hand. Joey brought the problem. Ricardo connected the team. Clayton built the tool. The first working version was ready in weeks. When the fund's attorneys saw a live demo, one said it was better than anything he had seen in the space.

By February 2026, SyncroDoc Systems was incorporated. Every feature in SPD Matrix exists because someone doing this work asked for it.

How it works
01

Your data stays yours

Every client gets a dedicated, isolated environment. Your plan documents, board minutes, and fee schedules never mingle with other clients' data. Built for fiduciary responsibility.

02

No shortcuts on context

A definition on page 2 can alter a clause on page 84. SPD Matrix processes the entire universe of your governing documents for every analysis. It reads everything, every time. No chunking, no guessing, no blind spots.

03

Every answer has a citation

Every extracted provision, modified amendment, and board decision is mapped directly to the source PDF with page and paragraph-level references. Click the link, verify the language.

04

Hundreds of hours in minutes

Parsing decades of amendments and competing plan structures is heavy work. SPD Matrix was built to handle massive document payloads. What costs your firm hundreds of hours in manual cross-referencing is handled in minutes with higher structural accuracy.
