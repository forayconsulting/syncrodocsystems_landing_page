Investment Policy
DECISION Board voted unanimously to approve a 5% allocation to the Cornerstone Real Estate Fund III, reducing the domestic equity allocation from 45% to 40%. Motion by Trustee Rodriguez, seconded by Trustee Chen. Implementation to begin Q2 2024.
Sources Board_Minutes_Mar2024.pdf, p. 4 Board_Minutes_Mar2024.pdf, p. 5
Funding Status
Actuary reported funded status at 87.3% as of January 1, 2024 valuation, up from 82.1% in prior year. Improvement attributed to strong equity returns and contribution discipline. Projected to reach 90% by 2026 under current assumptions.
Sources Board_Minutes_Mar2024.pdf, p. 2
Vendor Selection
DECISION Board authorized RFP for new third-party administrator. Current contract with PensionPro expires December 2024. Selection committee appointed: Trustees Chen, Williams, and Fund Counsel.
Sources Board_Minutes_Mar2024.pdf, p. 7
Plan Amendments
Counsel presented draft SMM #3 incorporating early retirement provisions and updated disability pension language. Board directed counsel to circulate to bargaining units for review before adoption at next quarterly meeting.
